## The calendar's anti-patterns (do NOT do)

1. **Don't ship more than 1 blog/week.** Quality > volume.
2. **Don't reply to every AI drama.** Pick 1 thread/week max.
3. **Don't run 2 simultaneous launches.** Show HN Jul 14. dglabs-eval Jul 25. Pre-orders Aug 26. Never two in one week.
4. **Don't run 2 wave threads in 1 week.** Snap, Meta, Apple, Ternus — pick the loudest. Move on.
5. **Don't post on Sunday.** Rest.
6. **Don't apologize for slow shipping.** Ship the receipts.
7. **Don't engage with overclaimers.** Quote the README, move on.
8. **Don't pitch paid media.** India press in Aug, founder-profile only.
