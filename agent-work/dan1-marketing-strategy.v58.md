## 5. The failure modes (v58, locked, 9 items, unchanged from v57)

1. **Pre-announcing the Q4 2026 launch without a public demo video** → inventory risk, brand credibility burn.
2. **Promising Omni-1B-Indic on a date we can't hit** → credibility burn on the only metric investors track.
3. **Letting Blurr creep into the marketing surface** → muddies the AI-glasses wedge. **Block.**
4. **Marketing `openwork` as a "personal AI" product without anchoring it to Dan Glasses** → forfeits the moat.
5. **Letting the Meta NameTag story do all the work for us** → reactive engagement, no compounding wedge.
6. **Letting the Snapdragon Reality Elite story do all the work for us** → same. Build the proactive wedge; the chip is just the receipt.
7. **Failing to ship the Day-0 punchlist today** → every other artifact is theoretical.
8. **Posting 10 threads in 48 hours** → the algorithm and the audience both punish the cadence. **2-3/day max.**
9. **Mistaking HN for the audience** → HN is for engineers, LinkedIn is for buyers, X is for the dev-tooling press. Match the channel to the message.
