## The reactive / breaking-news plan

When something breaks in the AI glasses world, we respond within 4 hours with a thread that:
1. Names the news
2. Compares it to our product in 1 sentence
3. Links to 1 proof artifact (code, demo, or commit)
4. Ends with 1 question to drive engagement

### Templates (v87)

**Template A — competitor launch:**
> [Competitor] just shipped [feature]. We shipped [our equivalent] [X days/weeks/months] ago.
> Here's the receipt: [link to code / demo / blog post].
> Question: would you rather have [their approach] or ours? Why?

**Template B — competitor bug / backlash:**
> [Competitor]'s [issue] is the exact problem we set out to solve.
> Our approach: [1 sentence, with proof link].
> Happy to share the implementation if anyone's interested.

**Template C — ecosystem trend:**
> Trend: [X] is becoming table stakes in AI glasses.
> We already do this: [proof link].
> Here's what's still missing from the ecosystem: [1 sharp observation].
