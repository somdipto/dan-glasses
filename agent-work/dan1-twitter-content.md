## 0. The rules (read first)

1. **Receipts > narrative.** Every post has a link, screenshot, demo, or number.
2. **One idea per post.** Don't cram. If it needs 2 ideas, it's a thread.
3. **Sharp, not snarky.** We can be direct, but we never punch down.
4. **No "excited to announce."** That's a content-farm tell. Replace with "shipping today:" or "just hit 1,000:" or "here's the data:"
5. **No hashtags.** Ever. Hashtags signal "trying too hard."
6. **Reply to every comment in the first 4 hours.** Algorithmic + human.
7. **Thread = 5 tweets max.** If you need more, write a Substack.
8. **We never quote-tweet our own tweet.** Cross-link in a follow-up instead.
