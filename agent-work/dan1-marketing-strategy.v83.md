## 3. The 6 positioning lines (v84 lock, unchanged from v83)

1. **Proactive, not reactive.** The glasses watch. They don't wait for a wake word. This is our answer to Siri-on-your-face.
2. **On-device by default.** No cloud. No Meta account. No Muse Spark dependency. Architectural answer to Meta-Stella.
3. **Open source, MIT.** Every daemon. Every model path. Every release signed. No covert update clause.
4. **Built in India, for the world.** Hindi/Tamil/Telugu/Bengali first. ₹12K / $145 founder. ₹4,999 / $60 student.
5. **Honest about the research.** danlab-multimodal is pre-RL. We say so. Eval ships Jul 25.
6. **Self-improving.** Dani (the brain) is recursive. Memoryd grows with the user.
